Whim Golf stands firmly at the intersection between art and sport as we seek to bring together otherwise fragmented audiences into a place of empathy and understanding. We seek to shift the perception of golf as a divisive game through clothing design. By refreshing the narrative around golf, we invite new individuals to participate in a truly timeless game that embodies admirable values.
