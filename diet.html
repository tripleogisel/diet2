LONGEVITY
A lot of people and companies triumph the 'new' - the ability to reinvent yourself over and over again, year after year... season after season. Fashion and automobiles are 2 industires that come to mind here. Less people and companies triumph the 'old' by way of long lasting products. It's all but guaranteed that EVERYTHING that ANYONE makes will become waste one day, but that drive to last as long is possible interests me. Some ~new brands (Noah) and some old brands (Visvim, below) champion lifetime products. Stuff that lasts not only in the sense that it's well-made and durable but also in the sense that it transcends the "years" and "seasons" that dictate so much of what we see produced for the world today. Better yet, these lifetime products evolve with us as a result of use. That's beautiful my Gs.
