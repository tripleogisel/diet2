03.30.17 - instagram shopping + unrelated hidden “fewer” feature

instagram launched or beta launched or something a new web view for eCommerce that displays photos in an almost-instagram like grid but it breathes a little bit more to facilitate shopping. when you open the shopping view one of the top things is a field to enter your email address and stay “up to date” on [insert account name here]’s latest whatever. i opened the web view with the link in bio, and assume it’s possible on a story’s “swipe up”, too. I had hoped it was possible to somehow go directly from an image to the “shopping” web view for that specific item… pretty sure that’s impossible today.

BUT! Alas, I discovered something totally hidden that is kinda useful. When you’re on the “bio” view for an account, you can press (hard AF!) on an image and bring up this all new page with an X in the top right hand corner next to “following”. if you tap the X it immediately delivers a quasi-“success” message explaining that instagram will show you “fewer” posts “like this”. Not sure what that means exactly, but it’s an interesting move in the world of instagram that’s built on a “you can follow me but i don’t have to follow you” model.

03.28.17 - Google Forms - Add Collaborator... "folks"

Google Forms “add collaborator” functionality, like most sharing-related features out there, makes like difficult. I have added 5 collaborators so far in groups of 2 or as even one at a time. I'm about to send this access out to someone new when I notice a pre-checked "Notify people" checkbox next to an "Add message" link, all below the "Email address" field for the newest collaborator I seek to add. If I enter a message, will everyone receive it, or is it just the new person? Who knows!

I decide to play it safe and uncheck the box.

But, when I finally attempt to add the collaborator I receive a cryptic error response about how “folks will have to sign in before they notice what I've shared”. Who are these folks?! Sign into what?! C’mon google, where else do you have folks out there?
