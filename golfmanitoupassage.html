Manitou Passage by Whim Golf

In August 2019 Whim Golf set out to play a gem of a course in Northern Michigan named Manitou Passage Golf Club. The course embodies what it means to be an outdoors person who plays golf. Its rolling hills, lush woodsy forests, and timeless buildings make it a must-play for any golfer who travels to Michigan to enjoy some of the best public golf in the US.
