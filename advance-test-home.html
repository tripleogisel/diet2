Why the Advance program?

Health, or ability to improve it, should be free. I developed “Advance” as a follow-up to my first program “Level-Up” in the Nike Training App.

However, for this program you needed to be in certain parts of the world or else you couldn’t get it. I also wanted to give you direct access to me to help you get the most out of these workouts.

So I created an open source website that allows anyone to participate and aim for improved health in these crazy times. I want you to text me and ask me questions/feedback. I’ll be hosting seminars I want you to join. Don’t have weights? That’s cool there will be bodyweight versions on Exercise Snacks.

I am putting the tools in your hands. Take control of your health. This is all free. LET’S GO.

How does it work?

It is an 8 week program that will help you improve your athleticism but also your health knowledge. Over the course of 8 weeks you will have 4 stages that are composed of 6 workouts. Every 2 weeks you will complete 6 workouts with options to engage in exercise on your rest/recovery days. Minimal equipment is required, you only need dumbbells and a jump rope.

This program is focused on improving multiple components of your fitness including mobility, strength, and endurance to make you a complete athlete while still being accessible for all skill levels.
